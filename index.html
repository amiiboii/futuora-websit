SMARTNAV

Intelligent Mobility, Redefined. See where you want to go. We will take you there.

For the millions living with severe motor impairments, a powered wheelchair is not just a device, it is independence. SMARTNAV takes that independence further than ever before.

SMARTNAV is an AI-powered autonomous wheelchair navigation system that gives users full, intuitive control through eye movement alone. Using non-invasive EOG (electrooculography) sensing, the system translates natural gaze direction into precise movement commands, eliminating the need for hand or voice input entirely.

At the core of SMARTNAV is a shared autonomy architecture. Onboard SLAM-based mapping and real-time obstacle detection work continuously in the background, so the wheelchair understands its environment and avoids hazards automatically, while the user stays in command of where they want to go.

Designed for real-world deployment, SMARTNAV integrates seamlessly into standard powered wheelchair platforms. No surgical procedures, no complex calibration routines, no specialist training required. Setup is straightforward and day-to-day use is natural from the very first session.

Target Users

Individuals with ALS, spinal cord injuries, muscular dystrophy, and other conditions limiting upper limb mobility, as well as care facilities and rehabilitation centres.
